Connecting Travelers with Trusted Travel Partners

Tourzin is a smart online tour vehicle booking platform that connects customers with verified travel operators and vehicle owners across Tamil Nadu, with a vision to expand throughout India.

Whether you're planning a family vacation, temple tour, corporate trip, wedding event, airport transfer, or an outstation journey, Tourzin makes it easy to discover, compare, and book the right vehicle — all in one place.

Our platform brings together tempo travellers, vans, mini buses, luxury coaches, buses, cars, and SUVs from trusted travel businesses, helping customers save time while giving travel operators a powerful digital presence.

Our Mission

To simplify vehicle booking by creating a transparent, reliable, and technology-driven platform that benefits both travelers and travel operators.

We believe finding the right vehicle should be as easy as booking a hotel or ordering food online.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted platform for tour vehicle discovery and booking by empowering local travel businesses with digital technology while delivering exceptional booking experiences to customers.

What We Do

Tourzin offers two booking models designed to serve different customer preferences.

Tourzin Managed Booking

For selected partners, Tourzin manages the complete booking process. Our team assists customers by:

  • Finding the right vehicle
  • Checking availability
  • Coordinating with travel operators
  • Confirming bookings
  • Providing booking support

This service is ideal for customers who prefer a guided and hassle-free booking experience.

Direct Listing

Tourzin also provides a marketplace where customers can discover travel operators and contact them directly. Customers can:

  • Compare available vehicles
  • View operator information
  • Contact the travel operator directly
  • Finalize pricing and trip details

This gives customers greater flexibility while helping travel businesses generate genuine enquiries.

Our Journey

Tourzin was created with a simple idea: finding a reliable tourist vehicle should not require dozens of phone calls, price negotiations, or uncertainty.

By combining technology with trusted travel partners, Tourzin aims to make booking tour vehicles faster, easier, and more transparent for everyone.

This is only the beginning of our journey. As we grow, we will continue expanding our network of travel partners, improving our technology, and introducing new features that make travel planning even more convenient.
