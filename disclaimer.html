Tourzin connects you directly with independent travel operators and vehicle owners. Please keep the following in mind before confirming any trip:

1. Have a clear conversation before the trip begins about:

  • Number of days
  • Destinations and routes
  • The driver's details

2. Ask the operator for:

  • Recent images or videos of the vehicle, or visit it directly
  • Route details
  • The final price bill

3. Also get the driver's contact details before the trip.

Tourzin does not own, operate, or inspect the vehicles listed on this platform and is not a party to the agreement between you and the operator. Always verify details directly with the operator before making any payment or starting your journey.
