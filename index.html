FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before booking a tour vehicle through Tourzin.

What is Tourzin?

Tourzin is an online listing and booking-facilitation platform that connects travelers with verified independent tour bus, van, tempo traveller, and coach operators across Tamil Nadu. Tourzin does not own or operate the vehicles — it helps you discover and contact operators directly.

How do I book a tour bus through Tourzin?

Sign in with your Google account, choose your district, and filter by bus type, seats, and availability. Once you find a bus you like, message the operator on WhatsApp or call them directly to confirm pricing and trip details.

Is Tourzin free to use?

Yes. Browsing listings and contacting operators through Tourzin is free for travelers. For select partners, Tourzin also offers a Managed Booking service where our team handles the process for you.

Does Tourzin own the buses?

No. Tourzin is a booking facilitation platform — every vehicle is owned and operated by an independent travel operator. Always verify vehicle condition, driver details, route, and final pricing directly with the operator before your trip.

Which districts in Tamil Nadu does Tourzin cover?

Tourzin currently lists tour buses, vans, and coaches across 25+ districts in Tamil Nadu, including Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Salem, Tiruchirappalli, and more — with a vision to expand across India.

How do I contact a bus operator?

Every bus listing includes the operator's phone number and a WhatsApp contact button, so you can reach them directly to ask questions, negotiate pricing, or confirm your booking.
